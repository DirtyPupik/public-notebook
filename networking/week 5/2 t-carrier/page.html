T-carrier - a type of boradband solution, transmit multiple phone calls over a single link, requires a dedicated line
invented by AT&T
T1 - transmition carrier 1 - the first specification. before T1, each call used 2 pairs of wire
T1 allowed up to 24 calls on a single twisted pair, up to 64 kbps for a call, and up to 1.5mbps per line
T3 is 28 T1 lines - gets to 44.7 mbps
T-carrier is less common today
