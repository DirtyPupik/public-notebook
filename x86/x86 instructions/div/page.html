div - unsigned divide instruction

Three forms:
  • Unsigned devide ax by r/m8 , al = quotient , ah = reminder
  • Unsigned devide edx:eax by r/m32 , eax = quotient , edx = reminder
  • Unsigned devide rdx:rax by r/m64 , rax = quotient , rdx = reminder
  • If divisor is 0, a divide by zero exception is raised.
