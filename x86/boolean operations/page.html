Boolean operations affect the majority of the status flags.
Bitwise boolean operations in C are preformed by:
  • & AND
  • | OR
  • ^ XOR - one or the other, but not both.
  • ~ NOT - One's complement negation. (Two's complement == One's complement + 1)

NOT is a single source/destination operator, <not rax> will store the result in rax.

XORing a value with itself will zero the result.
