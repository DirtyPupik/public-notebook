Privilege Rings

There are 4 Privilege Rings, 0 being the kernel.
de-facto, there are only two: kernel and userspace.

Protection rings
  • Are the interaction between the Requested Privilege Level (RPL), Descriptor Privilege Level (DPL) and the Current Privilege Level (CPL).
  • "The CPL is defined as the protection level of the currently executing code segment." (vol 3, sect 2.1.1).
  • CPL field are bits 0 and 1 of the CS register.
